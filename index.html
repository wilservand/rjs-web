About Us

Based in the strategic logistics hub of Batam, Indonesia, PT. Renindo Jaya Sukses is a dedicated PPJK Service provider committed to simplifying international trade for our clients.

We specialize in navigating the complexities of Indonesian customs regulations, with a specific focus on the unique procedures of the Batam Free Trade Zone (FTZ). Our team combines deep local knowledge with operational efficiency to ensure seamless import and export clearances.

At PT. Renindo Jaya Sukses, we bridge the gap between businesses and borders, ensuring your cargo moves swiftly, legally, and cost-effectively.
